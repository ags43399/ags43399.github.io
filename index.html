My Life With Ice-cream

Since I was a kid, my family and I have always bonded over ice-cream. My parents would let my siblings and I eat it as a post-dinner treat. We would eat it while watching movies, and TV shows together. We go to cute shops when we'd travel and get ice-cream. We'd stop at McDonalds on road trips and get vanilla ice-cream cones. My dad and brother's favorite ice-cream is Cookies & Cream. My sister's favorite ice-cream is Salted Caramel. My mom's favorite ice-cream is Neapolitan. My favorite ice-cream is Strawberry Cheesecake with Oreo from Dairy Queen. ice-cream is a diverse food that offers so many wonderful flavors that can appeal to a variety of people. I have always loved ice-cream and I always will and so will the rest of my family.

Reasons Why I Love Ice-cream:

  1. There are many flavors to choose from.
  2. There are dietary restriction options such as gluten free and dairy free.
  3. There are sweet and savory options such as cookie dough, or brownie batter, and strawberry or coffee.

Why Ice-cream is Amazing:

  • People, both young and old, love ice-cream.
  • It is a treat that anyone can enjoy because there are flavors that can appeal to everyone.
  • It is a food that you can use for celebrations such as birthdays, weddings, holidays, baby showers and much more.
  • It is perfect for all occasions whether studying, watching a movie, or spending time with family and friends.
  • You can combine it with a warm cookie, fresh brownies, or cake.
  • You can make ice-cream cake for a birthday celebration for those that don't like cake.
